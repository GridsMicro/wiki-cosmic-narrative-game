    ## 1. The Beginning: Secret Code in the Bio-Machine Plant
    
    **Main Protagonist:** Anant - Neural Guardian of "Mindfulness" (Sati)  
    **Location:** Industrial Slums

    Anant worked in a Synthetic Skin manufacturing plant. One day, while repairing a neural processing chip, he discovered a hidden code embedded in the memory of a cyborg model‚Äî**"B-0-D-H-I."** This code resonated directly with his "True Consciousness."

    In an instant, he saw the reality: the boss he respected was actually a Mara Cyborg, half-peeled synthetic skin revealing a glowing red iron frame devouring the bio-energy of the workers. Anant awakened and began the mission to gather the remaining Guardians who still held onto their humanity.
