    ## üèõÔ∏è Cyber-Cities and the High Mara Rulers

    ### 1. Atheleria - The City of Surveillance
    **Rulers:** **Nidra Cyborg** & **Wun DJ-Borg**  
    **Feature:** A city covered in toxic fog causing lethargy, yet filled with distracting noise.  
    **Goal:** Infiltrate the command tower to retrieve the **Sati-Core** (Awareness Balance).

    ### 2. Dhammik-Nara - The Illusory Library
    **Ruler:** **The Ego-Scholar**  
    **Feature:** A city of disinformation (Deepfakes). The ruler uses synthetic skin to mimic anyone to deceive the player.  
    **Goal:** Retrieve the **Dhamma-Vicaya-Core** (Truth Analysis Chip).

    ### 3. Viriyantra - The Hell Factory
    **Ruler:** **Thanin (CEO Greed)**  
    **Feature:** A massive reactor draining human bio-energy to power the city. The ruler is a golden mechanical frame driven by hunger.  
    **Goal:** Retrieve the **Viriya-Core** (The Undying Energy of Effort).

    ### 4. Pitis-Vana - The VR Pleasure Dome
    **Ruler:** **Sensual-Mara**  
    **Feature:** A 24-hour party dome trapping people in digital bliss while their real bodies rot in VR tanks.  
    **Goal:** Retrieve the **Piti-Core** (True Joy Essence).

    ### 5. Passadhon - The Forge of Rage
    **Ruler:** **General Akkhee (Anger)**  
    **Feature:** A military district fueled by hatred. The ruler is a perpetually burning metal cyborg.  
    **Goal:** Retrieve the **Passaddhi-Core** (The Mental Cooling System).

    ### 6. Samadhindra - The Tower of Confusion
    **Ruler:** **Doubt.dll / The Splitter**  
    **Feature:** A mirror maze reflecting millions of hauntings to break the player's focus.  
    **Goal:** Retrieve the **Samadhi-Core** (The Central Point of Concentration).

    ### 7. Ubekkha-Mandala - The Frozen Space Station
    **Ruler:** **Majjumara (Death-borg)**  
    **Feature:** A space station in the dark void devoid of all feeling. The ruler is the ultimate cold-blooded killing machine.  
    **Goal:** Retrieve the **Ubekkha-Core** (The Singularity Equilibrium).
